# Page Management

Manage your Facebook Pages connected to the business hub.

![Page Management](../assets/screenshots/06-nav-page-management.png)

## Features

- **View connected pages** — See all Facebook Pages linked to your businesses
- **Page metrics** — Follower count, engagement rates, post reach
- **Page settings** — Configure auto-responses, greeting text, ice breakers
- **Sync status** — Check if page data is up to date with Facebook

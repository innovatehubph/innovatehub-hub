# Products & Orders

Manage your product catalog and track customer orders.

## Products

![Products Page](../assets/screenshots/08-nav-products.png)

Browse and manage your product listings:
- **Add products** — Create new product entries with name, price, description, images
- **Edit products** — Update pricing, availability, and details
- **Search & filter** — Find products by name, category, or status
- **Sync with Facebook** — Push products to Facebook Shops catalog

![Products Data Table](../assets/screenshots/31-data-products.png)

## Orders

![Orders Page](../assets/screenshots/09-nav-orders.png)

Track customer orders placed through Facebook Shops:
- Order ID and status
- Customer details
- Products ordered
- Payment status
- Fulfillment tracking

![Orders Data Table](../assets/screenshots/32-data-orders.png)

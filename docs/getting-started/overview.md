# Overview

InnovateHub Business Hub is a centralized platform for managing Facebook business operations. It provides a modern, dark-themed dashboard with 14+ pages covering every aspect of Facebook business management.

## What You Can Do

### Business Management
Manage multiple Facebook Business accounts, each with their own pages, ad accounts, and product catalogs.

### Messenger Hub
View all conversations across your business pages. Read messages, see customer profiles, and track conversation history.

### Advertising
Create and manage Facebook ad campaigns. Monitor performance metrics, budgets, and audience targeting.

### Product Management
Maintain product catalogs synced with Facebook Shops. Track orders and manage inventory.

### Automation
Build bot flows for automated Messenger responses. Set up webhook handlers for real-time event processing.

### AI-Powered Development
Use the AI Workshop to describe features in natural language. Claude AI generates production-ready code, stages it for review, and deploys with one click.
